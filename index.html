Smart Waste Management System

Smart Waste Management is a system that uses sensors to monitor the waste levels in bins and alerts the waste management company when the bins are full. This system helps to reduce the number of overflowing bins and ensures that the waste is collected in a timely manner.

Our Services

Waste Monitoring

Our system uses sensors to monitor the waste levels in bins and alerts the waste management company when the bins are full.

Data Analysis

We analyze the data collected by the sensors to identify trends and patterns in waste generation and disposal.

Alert System

Our system sends alerts to the waste management company when the bins are full, so they can schedule waste collection.

About This project

This Smart Waste Management System was inspired by the growing problem of inefficient waste collection in urban areas. Living in a city where waste bins are often overflowing or empty yet still collected, I realized there was a need for a smarter, more efficient approach to waste management. Using IoT technology, we aim to optimize collection schedules and reduce the city's carbon footprint.

The project development timeline spanned over the course of 3 months, during which our team of developers and engineers collaborated to create a scalable solution. This project is part of the Holberton School Portfolio Project, where we applied our skills in full-stack development, systems design, and project management.
